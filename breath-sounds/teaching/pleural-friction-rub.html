10. 肋膜摩擦音

Pleural Friction Rub

聽診音檔Auscultation Audio
音檔改作自公開CC BY授權教學影片，詳細來源見「關於」頁。
Adapted from openly licensed (CC BY) educational recordings — full source details on the About page.

粗糙、類似皮革摩擦的不連續聲音，常伴隨吸氣時的銳利胸痛，是肋膜發炎的重要聽診線索。

A coarse, leathery, discontinuous sound often accompanied by sharp inspiratory chest pain — an important auscultatory clue for pleural inflammation.

🩺 聽診位置🩺 Auscultation Location

最常聽診於腋下與肺基部。

Most commonly heard in the axillary region and lung bases.

📊 聲學特性📊 Acoustic Characteristics

音調Pitch粗糙、低頻（grating）Coarse, low-frequency, grating quality
頻率Frequency未見獨立精確Hz數據，定性描述為low-frequencyNo independently reported precise Hz value; qualitatively described as low-frequency
時長／連續性Duration / Continuity不連續性；可能同時出現於吸氣和吐氣（biphasic），也可能僅吸氣可聞Discontinuous; may occur in both inspiration and expiration (biphasic), or on inspiration alone

⚙️ 產生機轉⚙️ Mechanism

傳統理論：臟層與壁層肋膜間正常有薄薄一層液體使兩層肋膜能無聲滑動，發炎時纖維滲出物讓肋膜表面變粗糙，滑動時產生摩擦聲。2022年版教科書提出另一觀點：兩層肋膜間通常仍有少量液體、不會真正直接接觸，聲音可能是增厚肋膜「形狀改變」所致，而非表面直接摩擦——此理論也能解釋為何大量肋膜積液時偶爾仍聽得到此聲音。

Traditional explanation: a thin layer of fluid normally allows the visceral and parietal pleura to slide silently; inflammation roughens the pleural surfaces with fibrinous exudate, producing friction as they slide. A 2022 textbook offers an alternative view: some fluid usually remains between the two pleural layers, meaning direct surface contact may not actually occur — the sound may instead arise from shape changes in thickened pleura. This theory also helps explain why the sound is occasionally still heard even with a large pleural effusion.

🔍 臨床意義🔍 Clinical Significance

肋膜發炎讓病人吸氣時特別痛，臨床上常看到病人刻意固定患側、減少胸壁活動以止痛。

Because pleural inflammation causes pain on inspiration, patients often splint the affected side and minimize chest wall movement to reduce discomfort.
