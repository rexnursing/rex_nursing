02. 肺泡音

Vesicular Breath Sounds

聽診音檔Auscultation Audio
音檔改作自公開CC BY授權教學影片，詳細來源見「關於」頁。
Adapted from openly licensed (CC BY) educational recordings — full source details on the About page.

聽診於雙側肺野大部分區域，是最典型、最常聽到的「正常」呼吸音，安靜柔和、幾乎是背景音等級。

Heard over most of both lung fields; the classic, most frequently encountered normal breath sound — quiet, soft, almost background in character.

🩺 聽診位置🩺 Auscultation Location

雙側肺野大部分區域（前、後、側胸壁）。

Most of both lung fields — anterior, posterior, and lateral chest wall.

📊 聲學特性📊 Acoustic Characteristics

音調Pitch柔和、低音調Soft, low-pitched
頻率Frequency200–600 Hz（Auscultation Skills 與 Murray & Nadel's 一致）200–600 Hz (consistent across Auscultation Skills and Murray & Nadel's)
時長／連續性Duration / Continuity吸氣清楚、持續整個吸氣期；吐氣立刻接上但很快淡出，約僅延續到吐氣前1/3；比例約3:1–4:1（吸長吐短）Clearly audible throughout inspiration; expiration follows immediately but fades quickly, lasting roughly only the first third of expiration. Ratio approx. 3:1–4:1 (inspiration longer than expiration).

⚙️ 產生機轉⚙️ Mechanism

並非源自肺泡本身開闔（肺泡進行氣體交換是無聲的過程），而是遠端支氣管的渦流氣流所產生。

Not actually generated by alveoli opening and closing (gas exchange in the alveoli is a silent process) — the sound originates from turbulent airflow in the distal bronchi.

🔍 臨床意義🔍 Clinical Significance

聲音越「無聊」（清楚、對稱、無額外雜音）代表肺部越健康；局部減弱或消失需考慮氣胸、肋膜積液、肺不張等。

The more unremarkable and symmetrical the sound, the healthier the lungs. Localized diminution or absence raises concern for pneumothorax, pleural effusion, or atelectasis.

📌 2022年版教科書指出「Vesicular」一詞其實是命名學上的誤稱——聲音並非來自肺泡（vesicle），書中已改稱「Normal breath sounds」。此為命名學觀點，不影響臨床上Vesicular一詞的教學使用。 📌 A 2022 textbook notes that "vesicular" is technically a misnomer, since the sound does not originate from the alveoli (vesicles) themselves; it now uses the term "normal breath sounds" instead. This is a naming-convention observation and does not affect the continued clinical use of the term "vesicular."
