11. 呻吟聲

Grunting

📝 文字說明・無聽診音檔可用（原因見下方說明）
📝 Text-only entry — no auscultation audio available (see note below)

新生兒吐氣時發出的短促呻吟聲，是身體自行製造呼氣末正壓（PEEP）以避免肺泡塌陷的代償機制，也是呼吸窘迫最早期的警訊之一。

A short grunting sound made by neonates during expiration — a compensatory mechanism generating self-produced positive end-expiratory pressure (PEEP) to prevent alveolar collapse, and one of the earliest warning signs of respiratory distress.

🩺 聽診位置🩺 Auscultation Location

不需聽診器，肉眼／耳朵即可察覺，屬臨床觀察徵象而非傳統聽診音。

Does not require a stethoscope — a clinically observed sign rather than a traditional auscultated sound.

📊 聲學特性📊 Acoustic Characteristics

音調Pitch不適用（非聽診分類音）Not applicable (not a stethoscope-based classification)
頻率Frequency無公開Hz數據No published Hz data
時長／連續性Duration / Continuity發生於吐氣期Occurs during expiration

⚙️ 產生機轉⚙️ Mechanism

新生兒在吐氣時刻意讓會厭部分關閉，使肺內維持一定正壓，藉此避免肺泡塌陷——是身體自行製造「呼氣末正壓（PEEP）」的代償機制。

Neonates partially close the epiglottis during expiration to maintain positive pressure within the lungs, preventing alveolar collapse — effectively a self-generated form of positive end-expiratory pressure (PEEP).

🔍 臨床意義🔍 Clinical Significance

常見於新生兒呼吸窘迫症候群（RDS），通常合併呼吸急促、鼻翼煽動、肋間凹陷、發紺等症狀一起出現。若身邊新生兒出現呻吟聲合併呼吸喘、臉色不佳，應立即就醫評估，不應視為正常哭鬧現象。

Commonly seen in neonatal respiratory distress syndrome (RDS), typically accompanied by tachypnea, nasal flaring, intercostal retractions, and cyanosis. Grunting in a neonate together with rapid breathing or poor color should prompt immediate medical evaluation — it should not be dismissed as ordinary fussing.

📌 此聲音在標準成人聽診教科書中幾乎找不到定義，主要屬新生兒科臨床觀察範疇，也沒有對應的聽診音檔可以示範——因此本系列中，呻吟聲是唯一一個純粹用文字說明、沒有音檔的項目。 📌 This sign is essentially absent from standard adult auscultation textbooks, as it belongs primarily to neonatal clinical observation. There is also no corresponding auscultation recording — making this the only entry in the series presented as text-only, without audio.
