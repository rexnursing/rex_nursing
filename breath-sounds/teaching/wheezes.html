異常呼吸音Abnormal / Adventitious Sound

07. 喘鳴音

Wheezes

聽診音檔Auscultation Audio

Adapted from openly licensed (CC BY) educational recordings — full source details on the About page.

高音調、連續性、帶音樂性的聲音，是氣喘等阻塞性疾病最具代表性的異常呼吸音。

A high-pitched, continuous, musical sound — the hallmark adventitious sound of obstructive airway disease such as asthma.

🩺 聽診位置🩺 Auscultation Location

胸腔內廣泛聽診；氣管上方聽診有時比肺野更敏感，評估氣喘病人時特別有用。

Widely heard throughout the chest; auscultation over the trachea is sometimes more sensitive than over the lung fields, which is especially useful when assessing asthma patients.

📊 聲學特性📊 Acoustic Characteristics

音調Pitch高音調、樂音樣（sinusoidal/musical）High-pitched, musical/sinusoidal quality
頻率Frequency主頻率 ≥400 Hz（ATS正式定義）Dominant frequency ≥400 Hz (per official ATS definition)
時長／連續性Duration / Continuity持續時間 >80ms（Bates'）／ATS定義 >250ms；連續性聲音，主要在吐氣，嚴重時吸氣也可聽到Duration >80 ms (Bates') / >250 ms per ATS definition; continuous sound, mainly expiratory, though may also be heard on inspiration in severe cases

⚙️ 產生機轉⚙️ Mechanism

主流理論為氣道壁在達到臨界流速時與氣流一起「顫動」（flutter theory），常見於支氣管痙攣或黏膜發炎造成的氣道窄縮。

The leading mechanism is the "flutter theory": airway walls oscillate together with airflow once a critical flow velocity is reached, typically from bronchospasm or mucosal inflammation narrowing the airway.

🔍 臨床意義🔍 Clinical Significance

常見於氣喘發作。重要臨床陷阱：當氣道窄縮到極限、氣流量低到幾乎聽不到聲音時，反而代表病情惡化到「寂靜胸」（silent chest），是需要立刻處理的臨床警訊——聲音變小不等於病情變好。

Commonly heard during asthma exacerbations. Important clinical pitfall: when airflow becomes so severely reduced that wheezing is barely audible, this may indicate a "silent chest" — a sign of critical worsening requiring immediate attention, not improvement.
