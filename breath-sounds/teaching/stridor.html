09. 哮鳴音

Stridor

聽診音檔Auscultation Audio

Adapted from openly licensed (CC BY) educational recordings — full source details on the About page.

高音調、樂音樣的連續性聲音，來自上呼吸道狹窄，嚴重時不需聽診器即可聽到，是呼吸道緊急狀況的重要警訊。

A high-pitched, musical, continuous sound arising from upper airway narrowing. In severe cases it is audible without a stethoscope, and represents an important warning sign of airway emergency.

🩺 聽診位置🩺 Auscultation Location

頸部（喉部至氣管上段）最清楚，這是與喘鳴音鑑別的關鍵位置。

Best heard over the neck (larynx to upper trachea) — the key location distinguishing it from wheezing.

📊 聲學特性📊 Acoustic Characteristics

音調Pitch高音調、樂音樣High-pitched, musical quality
頻率Frequency未見獨立精確Hz數據，定性描述為high-frequencyNo independently reported precise Hz value; qualitatively described as high-frequency
時長／連續性Duration / Continuity連續性聲音，吸氣時通常比吐氣更明顯、更持久，也可能為biphasicContinuous sound; typically more prominent and prolonged on inspiration, though may also be biphasic

⚙️ 產生機轉⚙️ Mechanism

上呼吸道（喉部到氣管上段）狹窄處產生的渦流氣流所致。病灶若為functional/dynamic，僅吸氣聽到；若為fixed（固定狹窄），吸吐氣皆可聽到。

Produced by turbulent airflow through a narrowed segment of the upper airway (larynx to upper trachea). A functional/dynamic lesion produces inspiratory-only stridor; a fixed lesion produces stridor throughout both inspiration and expiration.

🔍 臨床意義🔍 Clinical Significance

常見成因：會厭炎、哮吼（croup）、血管性水腫、異物哽塞、過敏性休克；亦可見於插管造成的氣管狹窄、拔管後呼吸道水腫。臨床上容易與喘鳴音混淆而延誤治療，關鍵鑑別在聽診位置——哮鳴音在頸部最清楚，喘鳴音則胸腔內比頸部明顯。意識清楚的病人常自行採高福勒氏坐姿以減輕呼吸費力感。

Common causes include epiglottitis, croup, angioedema, foreign body aspiration, and anaphylaxis, as well as post-intubation tracheal stenosis and post-extubation airway edema. Stridor is easily confused with wheezing, which can delay treatment — the key differentiator is location: stridor is loudest over the neck, while wheezing is louder over the chest. Alert patients often adopt a high-Fowler's position to ease the work of breathing.
