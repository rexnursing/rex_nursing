異常呼吸音Abnormal / Adventitious Sound

08. 鼾音

Rhonchi

聽診音檔Auscultation Audio
音檔改作自公開CC BY授權教學影片，詳細來源見「關於」頁。
Adapted from openly licensed (CC BY) educational recordings — full source details on the About page.

聽起來像打鼾的低音調連續性聲音，本質上是喘鳴音的低頻版本。

A low-pitched, continuous, snoring-like sound — essentially a lower-frequency variant of wheezing.

🩺 聽診位置🩺 Auscultation Location

主要於大氣道分布區域，吐氣時最明顯。

Heard mainly over large airways, most prominent during expiration.

📊 聲學特性📊 Acoustic Characteristics

音調Pitch低音調、打鼾樣音質（snoring quality）Low-pitched, snoring quality
頻率Frequency主頻率150–200 Hz（Bates'）；<200 Hz（Murray & Nadel's）Dominant frequency 150–200 Hz (Bates'); <200 Hz (Murray & Nadel's)
時長／連續性Duration / Continuity持續時間 >80ms；主要於吐氣，偶爾吸氣也可聽到Duration >80 ms; mainly expiratory, occasionally audible on inspiration as well

⚙️ 產生機轉⚙️ Mechanism

大氣道被分泌物或液體部分阻塞，加上fluid film破裂、氣道壁震動所致。1977年ATS分類系統將其歸類為「low-pitched wheezes」，與喘鳴音同屬連續性聲音，僅頻率不同。

Caused by partial obstruction of large airways with secretions or fluid, combined with rupture of fluid films and vibration of the airway wall. The 1977 ATS classification system categorizes rhonchi as "low-pitched wheezes" — the same continuous-sound family as wheezes, differing only in frequency.

🔍 臨床意義🔍 Clinical Significance

與喘鳴音最大的臨床差異：鼾音常因咳嗽或抽痰而改變或消失，這也是判斷分泌物是否鬆動、是否需要抽痰的實用線索。

The key clinical distinction from wheezes: rhonchi frequently change or disappear after coughing or suctioning — a practical clue for whether secretions are mobile and whether suctioning may be needed.

📌 學界對「是否仍需要Rhonchi這個獨立術語」仍有爭論，部分學者主張直接統一稱為「low-pitched wheezes」較精簡，但臨床上Rhonchi一詞仍普遍使用。 📌 There is ongoing academic debate over whether "rhonchi" should remain a distinct term, with some scholars favoring the more streamlined "low-pitched wheezes." Clinically, however, "rhonchi" remains in widespread use.
