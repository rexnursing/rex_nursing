01. 氣管音

Tracheal Breath Sounds

📝 文字說明・無聽診音檔可用（原因見下方說明）
📝 Text-only entry — no auscultation audio available (see note below)

正常呼吸音中最大聲、音調最高的一種，聽診於氣管本身的位置，是評估中央至上段氣道通暢度的重要參考音。

The loudest and highest-pitched of the normal breath sounds, heard directly over the trachea. Serves as a useful reference for assessing patency of the central-to-upper airway.

🩺 聽診位置🩺 Auscultation Location

胸骨兩側第2–4肋間（前）；脊椎旁第3–6肋間（後）；插管病人聽診氣管音有助判斷上呼吸道是否通暢。

2nd–4th intercostal spaces lateral to the sternum (anterior); 3rd–6th intercostal spaces alongside the spine (posterior). In intubated patients, a clear tracheal sound helps confirm upper-airway patency.

📊 聲學特性📊 Acoustic Characteristics

音調Pitch音調高，音量最大High pitch, loudest of all breath sounds
頻率Frequency200–2,000 Hz（Auscultation Skills, 2014）；另一文獻記載為75–1,600 Hz，於900Hz處明顯衰減（Murray & Nadel's, 2022）200–2,000 Hz (Auscultation Skills, 2014); alternatively reported as 75–1,600 Hz with marked attenuation around 900 Hz (Murray & Nadel's, 2022)
時長／連續性Duration / Continuity吐氣略長於吸氣，比例約1:2–1:3（Bates'：吸吐氣時長幾乎相等）Expiration slightly longer than inspiration, ratio approx. 1:2–1:3 (Bates' describes inspiration and expiration as nearly equal in length)

⚙️ 產生機轉⚙️ Mechanism

氣流通過氣管（大氣道）時產生的渦流亂流所致，與支氣管音同源，僅聽診位置不同。

Produced by turbulent airflow through the trachea (a large, central airway) — the same underlying mechanism as bronchial sounds, differing only in auscultation site.

🔍 臨床意義🔍 Clinical Significance

插管後聽到清楚的氣管音，代表中央到上段氣道通暢；臨床上常作為聽診「基準音」，用來與其他部位的呼吸音比較。

A clear tracheal sound after intubation suggests the central-to-upper airway is patent. Clinically useful as a reference sound for comparison with breath sounds heard elsewhere.
