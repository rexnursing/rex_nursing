04. 支氣管音

Bronchial Breath Sounds

聽診位置決定一切的呼吸音——出現在氣管附近是正常，出現在周邊肺野卻是警訊。

A sound whose significance depends entirely on location — normal near the trachea, but a warning sign if heard in the lung periphery.

🩺 聽診位置🩺 Auscultation Location

正常：氣管兩側附近。異常：任何周邊肺野（原本該是肺泡音的區域）。

Normal: near the trachea. Abnormal: anywhere in the lung periphery, where vesicular sounds are normally expected.

📊 聲學特性📊 Acoustic Characteristics

音調Pitch大聲、粗糙，音調偏高（Bates'：relatively high；另一文獻：medium-pitched，兩者皆為主觀描述，非精確數據衝突）Loud, harsh, relatively high-pitched (Bates' describes it as "relatively high"; Murray & Nadel's uses "medium-pitched" — both are qualitative descriptions rather than conflicting hard data)
頻率Frequency未見獨立精確Hz數據No independently reported precise Hz range in the source texts
時長／連續性Duration / Continuity吐氣時間比吸氣長，吸吐氣之間有短暫靜默空檔Expiration longer than inspiration, with a brief silent gap between the two phases

⚙️ 產生機轉⚙️ Mechanism

氣流通過大氣道（氣管、主支氣管）的渦流亂流所致。異常出現於周邊，代表原本會吸收/分散聲音的含氣肺組織，被實變（consolidation）、肺不張（atelectasis）或纖維化疤痕組織取代，使聲音得以完整傳導。

Produced by turbulent airflow through large central airways. When heard peripherally, it indicates that air-filled lung tissue — which normally dampens sound — has been replaced by consolidation, atelectasis, or fibrotic scarring, allowing the sound to transmit more directly.

🔍 臨床意義🔍 Clinical Significance

異常支氣管音常見於肺炎、肺不張、肺纖維化；常伴隨觸覺震顫增加、耳語聲傳導過度清晰（whispered pectoriloquy）、egophony、bronchophony等理學檢查發現。

Abnormal bronchial breath sounds are commonly associated with pneumonia, atelectasis, and pulmonary fibrosis, and often accompany increased tactile fremitus, whispered pectoriloquy, egophony, and bronchophony on physical exam.
