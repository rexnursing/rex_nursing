06. 粗爆裂音

Coarse Crackles

聽診音檔Auscultation Audio
音檔改作自公開CC BY授權教學影片，詳細來源見「關於」頁。
Adapted from openly licensed (CC BY) educational recordings — full source details on the About page.

比細爆裂音更響亮、更低沉的「濕答答」爆裂聲，常隨咳嗽而改變。

Louder, lower-pitched, "wetter"-sounding crackles than fine crackles, often changing in character after coughing.

🩺 聽診位置🩺 Auscultation Location

依病灶而異，COPD、支氣管擴張症等疾病中常較廣泛分布。

Location varies with the underlying condition; often more diffusely distributed in COPD and bronchiectasis.

📊 聲學特性📊 Acoustic Characteristics

音調Pitch響亮、低音調Loud, low-pitched
頻率Frequency約350 HzApprox. 350 Hz
時長／連續性Duration / Continuity每次約15–30毫秒，比細爆裂音更長；從吸氣早期延續到吐氣（biphasic）Approx. 15–30 ms per crackle, longer than fine crackles; begins in early inspiration and continues into expiration (biphasic)

⚙️ 產生機轉⚙️ Mechanism

氣體斷斷續續通過分泌物產生的「啵啵」聲，機轉為氣道因分泌物間歇性開閉（boluses of gas passing through airways as they open and close intermittently）。不因姿勢改變而消失，但會因咳嗽而改變或消失，可傳導至口腔附近。

A "popping" sound produced as gas passes intermittently through secretion-laden airways that open and close. Does not change with body position, but often changes or resolves after coughing, and may be audible near the mouth.

🔍 臨床意義🔍 Clinical Significance

常見於COPD、氣喘、支氣管擴張症、肺炎、心衰竭。肺炎恢復期病人的粗爆裂音會逐漸變細、出現時間往後移，是肺部逐漸康復的聲音證據。

Commonly heard in COPD, asthma, bronchiectasis, pneumonia, and heart failure. In patients recovering from pneumonia, coarse crackles gradually become finer and shift later in the respiratory cycle — an acoustic sign of clinical improvement.
