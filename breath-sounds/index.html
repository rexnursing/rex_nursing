📚 內容範圍📚 Scope

本站內容整理自三本呼吸音／身體評估教科書的交叉比對：Auscultation Skills: Breath & Heart Sounds（5th ed., 2014）、Bates' Guide to Physical Examination and History Taking（12th ed., 2017）、Murray & Nadel's Textbook of Respiratory Medicine（7th ed., 2022），並輔以新生兒呼吸窘迫相關臨床指引。所有數據與敘述皆標明出處，文獻之間若有數據差異，會在該頁面同時列出、註明各自來源，不擅自選擇單一版本。

Content is synthesized from a cross-comparison of three auscultation and physical-assessment textbooks: Auscultation Skills: Breath & Heart Sounds (5th ed., 2014), Bates' Guide to Physical Examination and History Taking (12th ed., 2017), and Murray & Nadel's Textbook of Respiratory Medicine (7th ed., 2022), supplemented by neonatal respiratory distress clinical guidelines. Every figure and statement is cited; where sources disagree, both are presented side by side with attribution rather than silently picking one.

⚠️ 本站內容為衛教與學習參考用途，不構成醫療建議。實際臨床判斷請依照專業訓練與醫囑進行。 ⚠️ This site is for educational and reference purposes only and does not constitute medical advice. Clinical decisions should follow professional training and physician guidance.
